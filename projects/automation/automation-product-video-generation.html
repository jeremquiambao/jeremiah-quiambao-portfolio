AI Automation Case Study

Product Video Generation

A multimedia workflow that takes product details and uploaded photos, creates AI visuals, generates a short video, polls for completion, and sends the finished output.

Workflow Type: n8n Automation Category: AI Systems Format: Demo Project

Overview

A multimedia workflow that takes product details and uploaded photos, creates AI visuals, generates a short video, polls for completion, and sends the finished output.

Problem

Turning product information into promotional creative usually requires multiple production tools and time-intensive manual handoffs.

Solution

I created an n8n pipeline that accepts form input, uploads assets, generates an image prompt, creates product visuals, sends them into a video-generation step, polls for completion, and delivers the finished result by email.

Tools Used

n8n, OpenAI, ImgBB, Runway, Google Drive, Gmail

Workflow Screenshot

Key Features

  • Form and file upload intake
  • AI image generation
  • Automated video rendering flow
  • Polling and final asset delivery

Business Use Case

Useful for ecommerce teams, agencies, and product marketers that need faster asset creation for launches, promos, and catalog campaigns.
