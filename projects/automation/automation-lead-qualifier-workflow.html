Lead Qualifier: Gmail & Slack Automation

A lead qualification workflow that processes form submissions, organizes lead details, sends follow-up emails, updates Airtable, and alerts the team through Slack.

Workflow Type: Business Automation Category: Lead Management Format: Client Project

Overview

The Lead Qualifier workflow was built to automate the early lead handling process. It captures form submissions, organizes lead information, sends a follow-up email, updates a database, and notifies the team in Slack.

Problem

Businesses often receive leads from forms but manually review, qualify, save, and route them. This can delay follow-ups and create inconsistent lead management.

Solution

I built an automation that connects form submissions to Airtable, Gmail, Slack, and Calendly. The workflow helps teams respond faster and keep lead information organized.

Tools Used

Make.com, Tally, Airtable, Gmail, Slack, Calendly

Workflow Screenshot

Key Features

  • Form-based lead capture
  • Automated lead organization
  • Gmail follow-up email
  • Slack team notification
  • Airtable database update
  • Calendly link integration

Business Use Case

Useful for agencies, consultants, and service businesses that want to reduce manual lead handling and improve response speed.
