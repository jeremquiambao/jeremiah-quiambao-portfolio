Website & Digital Product Case Study

Toby Maguire Website

A personal brand website built to promote coaching services, showcase an eBook, and support a simple digital product purchase journey.

Type: Website Development Category: Coaching / Digital Products Format: Client Project

Overview

Toby Maguire website was created for a personal brand offering coaching services and digital products. The website was structured to present offers clearly and guide visitors toward booking or purchasing.

Problem

The brand needed a website that could explain coaching services, promote an eBook, and support users through a simple online buying journey.

Solution

I built the website using Squarespace, organized the service and product sections, and prepared the structure for e-commerce and digital product selling.

Tools Used

Squarespace, Mailchimp, Acuity Scheduling, Stripe, Custom CSS

Website Screenshot

Key Features

  • Coaching service presentation
  • eBook product section
  • Payment-ready structure
  • Personal brand layout
  • Simple visitor journey

Business Use Case

Useful for coaches, consultants, and creators who need a website to promote services, sell digital products, and build personal brand credibility.
